Employment and Labour Law

Strul Law provides expert advice in the areas of Employment and Labour law. Strul Law can help employers and employees with labour and employment matters by providing proactive advice that will ensure compliance with legal and regulatory frameworks, promote best practices, and help avoid costly litigation. We specialise in providing strategies that help our clients achieve timely, effective results with minimal hassle.

We can help you with matters pertaining to Human Rights, Occupational Health and Safety, the WSIB, Workplace Investigations, Workplace Privacy and FIPPA compliance, Terminations (Wrongful Dismissals, Constructive Dismissals, etc.), and matters governed by Collective Agreements.

We have expertise providing advice to large, medium-sized, and small employers from a variety of sectors and employees from all backgrounds.
